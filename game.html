Game Rules

  • Random letters (3 to 6) will be generated.
  • You can guess any English word made only from those letters.
  • You don't need to use all letters.
  • Points awarded based on word length:
    • 2 or 3 letters: 1 point
    • 4 letters: 2 points
    • 5 letters: 3 points
    • 6 letters: 4 points
  • Words validated via an online dictionary.
  • Found words appear below with definitions.
